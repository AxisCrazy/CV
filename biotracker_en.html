BIOTRACKER

AI Engineering & Data Pipelines

Project Architecture

BioTracker automates wildlife identification through Computer Vision. The technical core lies in the ability to process video streams and convert them into structured metadata for scientific research.

Vision (Deep Learning)

Using TensorFlow for training convolutional neural networks focused on species classification.

Data Processing

Implementation of Scikit-learn for data scaling, normalization, and noise reduction within the dataset.

Storage Strategy

A hybrid solution was implemented to maximize efficiency:

  • SQL (MariaDB): Referral integrity management for taxonomy and validated sighting records.

  • NoSQL (WIP): High-speed logging structure for temporal metadata and unstructured vision data.
